source("functions.R")

Allele appearnce

The group of IGHV1-69/IGHV1-69D includes 20 alleles, 20 out of the alleles are functional.

For each allele we counted the number of appearances across the population, any appearance was considered valid.

allele_appearance(data, g_group, allele_db)

Sequence depth

To examine the potential cutoff we observed the sequence depth for each allele

tagList(sequence_depth(data, g_group, allele_db))

Observations

For this group weve seen a single allele, hence the general summary statistics do not apply here and were omitted.
